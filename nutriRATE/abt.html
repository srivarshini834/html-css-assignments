What is NutriRate?

NutriRate is a simple, beginner-friendly website that helps you understand what’s in your food and gives it a health score out of 10. From fruits to fast food, we break it down clearly and quickly.

Why NutriRate?

Food labels can be confusing. NutriRate gives you quick facts, honest ratings, and clear comments to help you make smarter eating choices — without needing any nutrition degree.

How It Works

Every food page includes: Basic nutrition info (calories, carbs, protein, fat, etc.) A health score out of 10 A short, simple comment about the food The scores are based on general nutrition logic, not official dietary guidelines
